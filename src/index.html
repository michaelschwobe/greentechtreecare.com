About

Services

Plant Health Care

In an urban setting it may be difficult to maintain healthy trees. Insect and disease problems in most cases are due to a trees inability to fight off the problem on its own. Diagnosing and treating the problem, not the affect is the key to keeping your trees and shrubs healthy. The tree care industry has made advancement in the way trees can be treated. Many of which avoid spraying chemicals into the air that can drift off target. At Green Tech, we only use these advanced application techniques’ in our treatments. They have had proven results as well as the smallest impact on the environment the industry has to offer.

Pruning

As trees grow, they need direction. This can be accomplished through many types of pruning techniques. Specialized training and equipment, as well as an eye for what really needs to be done are the tools that we use to complete our work. Pruning trees not only enhances the appearance of your landscape, it helps to maintain its health and vitality. Pruning Services we provide:

Crown Cleaning
The selective removal of one or more of the following items: dead, dying, or diseased branches, weak branches and water sprouts.
Structural
To establish a strong branch scaffold system.
Crown Raising
The removal of the lower branches to provide clearance.
Vista
The selective thinning of framework limbs or specific area of the crown to allow a view of an object from a predetermined spot.
Thinning
The selective removal of branches to increase light penetration, air movement and to reduce weight.
Crown Restoration
Should improve the structure, form and appearance of trees that have been severely headed, vandalized or storm damaged.
Crown Restoration or Shaping
Decrease the height and/ or spread of a tree. Consideration should be given to the ability of the species to sustain this type of pruning.

Removal

Removing a tree on your property may become necessary for a variety of reasons. Do to the sheer size or location of a tree, its removal may be dangerous, involve large equipment, and take a great deal of time. We will discuss with you in detail what we will be doing to remove your tree as well as keep the rest of your landscape from being damaged in the process. This allows the homeowner to be involved in the planning and have full knowledge of the operation at hand.

Stump Grinding

Stumps left from removed trees can be unsightly and hazardous. We will grind out any stump to a depth of ten to twelve inches below grade, unless a utility or fixed object stands in the way. By Wisconsin state law Digger’s Hotline will be contacted by us prior to any bellow ground work on a client’s property. As always, we strive to meet the needs of our clients by providing a service that includes the complete removal of a stump as well as filling the hole with top soil, applying grass seed and starter fertilizer as needed.

Support Systems

Removing a tree that you value do to structural defects is not the only option available. A tree that has an elevated risk of failure can have a support system installed. When assessed and installed correctly, these cables and braces aid in reducing the potential of failure and can extend the life of a tree. Be aware that it is an industry standard to have a tree pruned prior to any support system being installed and such will be advised to you by our arborist.

Storm Damage & Wound Tracing

When trees become damaged or fail completely due to wind, ice, snow, lightening or construction, they will most likely need immediate attention due to the hazards they create. Our staff is experienced in safely removing large limbs or whole trees under these dangerous conditions as well as repairing the wounds that damaged trees have sustained.
